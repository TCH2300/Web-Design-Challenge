Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this website is to showcase how certain weather conditions (x) relate to the latitude of a randonomly selected group of cities. The cities analayzed (n=562) are all across the world of varying distances from the equator. The data for anlayzation was collected using Citipy and OpenWeather API.

The following analyses were completed:

  • Latitude vs. Cloudiness
  • Latitude vs. Humidity
  • Latitude vs. Temperature
  • Latitude vs. Windspeed
